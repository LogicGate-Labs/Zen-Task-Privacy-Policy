Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Zen Task ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We believe your focus and sanctuary should be private. Zen Task does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII).

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use the app.
  • We do not track your location.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All customization and task data is stored locally on your device using Local Storage. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible by us.

Specific data stored locally includes:

  • Mood History: Your selected "Current Vibe" and preferences.
  • Theme Preferences: Your selected color palette from the Theme Garden.
  • App Settings: Preferences such as volume levels.

Note: If you uninstall the app or clear your browser/app cache, this data will be deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses minimal third-party services to function:

  • Audio Streaming: Focus sounds and ambient tracks are streamed directly from secure content delivery networks (such as GitHub Raw User Content). These requests function like standard web traffic and do not track your personal identity.
  • No Ads: We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics software to track your behavior inside the app.
